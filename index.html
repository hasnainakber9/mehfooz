Why Now?

In conflict-prone regions with limited access to reliable news, social media becomes the de facto source of information. This reliance accelerates the spread of false narratives, exacerbating existing tensions. The need for a trust-based intervention has never been more critical.

"We are addressing the root causes of digital vulnerability to create resilient communities capable of navigating a complex information landscape with confidence."
