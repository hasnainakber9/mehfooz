FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who is this platform for? +

Mehfooz is designed for everyone in communities vulnerable to misinformation, including students, community members, local leaders, and educational institutions in regions like Gilgit Baltistan. Our goal is to make digital literacy accessible to all, regardless of technical skill level.

How is your content created and verified? +

Our educational materials are developed in collaboration with local leaders, researchers, and students to ensure cultural relevance and accuracy. We uphold the highest standards of integrity, fact-checking our content rigorously to provide trustworthy information.

What makes Mehfooz different from other fact-checking websites? +

While fact-checking is a component, our primary focus is on proactive education and empowerment. We differentiate ourselves through hyper-localized content in indigenous languages, deep community integration with local leaders, and innovative technology like the MehfoozBot to provide personalized, preventative learning experiences.

How can I get involved or support your mission? +

We are always looking for partners, investors, community leaders, and volunteers. If you are passionate about combating misinformation and promoting digital literacy, please reach out to us via the contact form below. We'd love to explore how we can collaborate.
