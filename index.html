About Mehfooz Internet

Cultivating Digital Wisdom

Our Mission

To empower the people of Gilgit Baltistan with trustworthy, lifelong learning opportunities that promote online safety and digital literacy through culturally-tailored educational content and community engagement.

Our Vision

To cultivate a digitally literate society in Gilgit Baltistan where individuals can confidently navigate the digital landscape, identify misinformation, and make informed decisions in the digital age.

Core Values

Collaboration

Building partnerships with local leaders, researchers, and students

Integrity

Upholding highest standards of accuracy in educational materials

Inclusivity
